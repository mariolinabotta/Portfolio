Urban regeneration of the area of the former Fruit and Vegetables Market @Lucca

The project area is located at south-east of the city walls that surround the historic centre of Lucca and hosts the old fruit and vegetable market delimited to the south by the railway line, which represents a very strong visual barrier, and to the north by the driveway ring that surrounds the walls; lastly to the east we find the building of the former Public Butchers, now the seat of the State Archives.


The aim of the project is to establish a consistent system with the urban structure in which it must fit and which can be connected with intrinsic features of the city: the encounter among the development directions of the surroundings of the project area has led to the consolidation of a system of main axes, one of which represents the penetration axis which connects directly with the circular avenues and which then puts in communication the project area with the old town. The other axis runs through the area in transversal direction, combining in itself the prevailing directions oh the surrounding.
The other fundamental characteristic of the intervention is the creation of a system of squares that can be connected by both pedestrian and driveway paths, able to represent the essence of the historical squares of Lucca outside the walls, where today they are almost completely absent.


The form that predominates in the design remains however the hemicycle one that constitutes the fruit and vegetable market, a very strong sign in the morphology of this area and that stands in contrast with the particular shape of the square of the Amphitheatre, located in the historic city.
The semicircular shape leads to a concentric development of the lot, in fact all this area has been treated with predominance of the theme of the circle“”: the portico existing was flanked to an urban plate on two levels, of which the lower has special curved openings capable of creating permeability between The inside and the outside; This trick allows people to discover the square by coming from the rear front of the semicircular body and to lie in the square on which overlook commercial activities, refreshments and parking spaces shaded. On the plate lies a path that follows that of the porch below: this has been articulated in three bodies, with circular pattern, which have been truncated and rotated in three different directions to give life to a dynamic system, involving and contemporary.


Since the layout of the old market is at the heart of the project, the rest keeps on sober and functional tones, going to create a system of closed different sizes capable of hosting public and semi-public spaces of different nature. The volumetric articulation leads to emphasize the main axes, where they are located seven-storey and commercial buildings on the ground floor, while the rest of the system is developed instead on a maximum height of five floors, mainly designed to living quarters and living quarters.


The green system has a central role in the project: the aim is to reconnect the green ring that surrounds the walls through a system of tree-lined avenues and a succession of squares that alternate green spaces with paved spaces. It affects the redevelopment of the two factories that arise near the area of project allowed to extend the green path going to create a new urban park near the railway line. This insert try to fencing the railway, an element that determines for a nature a break on the territory and is usually associated with areas of degradation.
